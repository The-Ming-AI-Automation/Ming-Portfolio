Core features

Quick Bookings

Customers book online — appointment created in Google Calendar automatically.

Instant Quotes

Submit device issue → AI estimates next steps; customers receive immediate estimate email.

Post-Service Feedback

After completion, system triggers an AI-summarized survey and review prompt if satisfaction is high.

Automation in action

  1. Customer fills the booking form (Google Form).
  2. n8n picks up new response → creates Google Calendar event + adds row to Google Sheet.
  3. Vapi/OpenAI generates confirmation email and estimated quote → sent via Gmail.
  4. Reminder sent 24hrs before; after service, feedback captured and processed.
